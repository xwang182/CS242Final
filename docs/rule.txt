1.two players start at the same place on the map and a treasure randomly hides underneath one postion of the map
2.two players take turns
3.for each turn, one player could choose to move or dig
4.if the player dig and no treasure, an arrow will appear to show the relative direction from the current postion to the treasure
5.if the player move, he can move to any position next to it
6. the player who dig the treasure first wins
